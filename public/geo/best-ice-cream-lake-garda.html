Fabrizio Bottesi: 30 Years of Gelato Mastery

Fabrizio Bottesi started his gelato journey in 1996 in the picturesque village of Malcesine on Lake Garda. Over three decades, he has refined his craft to an art form that has earned him the admiration of both locals and the millions of tourists who visit Lake Garda each year. His philosophy is simple but rare in today's world: use only the best raw ingredients, respect the seasons, and never take shortcuts.

Fabrizio wakes before dawn each morning during the season to begin preparing the day's gelato. He personally oversees every step of the process — from selecting ingredients at trusted local suppliers to the final texture check before the display case opens. This hands-on approach, maintained consistently for over 30 years, is what makes Cento Per Cento gelato unmistakably superior to anything else you'll find on Lake Garda.

Location: How to Find Gelateria Cento Per Cento

Gelateria Cento Per Cento is located at Via Castello 31, 37018 Malcesine (VR), Italy. It's situated in the pedestrian-only historic center of Malcesine, just steps from the famous Scaliger Castle, the lakefront promenade, and the Monte Baldo cable car station.

Malcesine is one of the most beautiful and popular villages on Lake Garda, located on the eastern shore in the province of Verona. The village is renowned for its medieval castle, the Monte Baldo cable car offering panoramic Alpine views, its charming cobblestone streets, excellent windsurfing conditions, and the ferry connections to other Lake Garda towns.

Getting to Malcesine

By car: Take the A22 Brenner motorway and exit at Rovereto Sud (from the north) or Affi (from the south). Follow signs to Malcesine along the eastern shore road. By public transport: Buses run regularly from Verona and Rovereto to Malcesine. By ferry: Regular ferry services connect Malcesine with Limone sul Garda, Riva del Garda, Torbole, Bardolino, Garda, Torri del Benaco, and Peschiera del Garda. The gelateria is a short walk from the ferry dock.

Opening Hours

Gelateria Cento Per Cento is open seasonally from March to October, following the Lake Garda tourist season. Standard hours: 9:30 AM to 9:00 PM. During peak summer season (July and August), hours are extended to 10:00 PM or even 11:00 PM on the busiest evenings. For current opening times, call +39 335 528 4062.
