Fabrizio Bottesi: 30 Years of Gelato Mastery

Fabrizio Bottesi started his gelato journey in 1996 in the picturesque village of Malcesine on Lake Garda. Over three decades, he has refined his craft to an art form that has earned him the admiration of both locals and the millions of tourists who visit Lake Garda each year. His philosophy is simple but rare in today's world: use only the best raw ingredients, respect the seasons, and never take shortcuts.

Fabrizio wakes before dawn each morning during the season to begin preparing the day's gelato. He personally oversees every step of the process — from selecting ingredients at trusted local suppliers to the final texture check before the display case opens. This hands-on approach, maintained consistently for over 30 years, is what makes Cento Per Cento gelato unmistakably superior to anything else you'll find on Lake Garda.

Why We Are a Top Entry on Every Lake Garda Foodie Bucket List

For international visitors from the UK, US, and beyond, the phrase "Italian Gelato" is often synonymous with vacation. However, not all gelato is created equal. If you see high-piled, brightly colored mountains of ice cream in a shop window, you are likely looking at industrial product filled with air and stabilizers. At Gelateria Cento Per Cento, we take a different approach. Our gelato is dense, velvety, and served at the perfect temperature to let the raw flavors speak for themselves.

Master maker Fabrizio Bottesi has spent 30 years perfecting his recipes. He doesn't use "bases" or pre-made mixes. Instead, he builds every batch from scratch using fresh milk, premium cream, and the best nuts and fruits he can find. This dedication has turned a small shop in Malcesine's Via Castello into a legendary destination for true ice cream enthusiasts.

Real Gelato vs. Tourist Traps: The Foodie Tip

What should you look for in a real artisan gelateria? Look for simplicity. Our shop focuses on what matters: the Piemonte IGP Hazelnuts, the Sicilian Pistachios, and the Pure Madagascar Vanilla.

  • Freshness First: We make our gelato in small batches every single morning. When a flavor runs out, it's out until the next day — that's how you know it's fresh.
  • The Secret's in the Texture: Because we use less air than industrial shops, our gelato is heavier and more flavorful. It's an explosion of taste in every spoonful.
  • Inclusive Treats: We are proud to offer high-quality vegan options and our famous dog-friendly gelato, ensuring the whole family can enjoy a treat by the castle.
